About HackNUthon

With the great success of HackNUThon 3.0, team CSI is back with another banger!! We present to you HackNUThon 4.0, one of the biggest flagship events of our club. HackNUThon is a great platform to showcase your development skills, learn new technologies and develop connections among your peers, mentors and other eminent personalities.

After a gap of 2 years, this year HackNUThon 4.0 will be held in complete offline mode for a span of 2 days. Each team has to select a track from the given list of tracks. The team needs to propose a solution for the related problem statement.

Why Sponsor Us?

Exposure to Young, Intelligent and Brilliant Minds

Nirma University has one of the best pool of enthusiastic student developers who are always ready to showcase their talents. Supporting this event will not only be a great marketing activity but also a super motivating factor for the students.

Advertising your product to over a 500 participants

In today's social media world, students have the best connections over several applications. HackNUThon is one of the best way to promote the company among students of Nirma University and other participating universities.

A chance to promote innovation and opportunities

Over the years, HackNUthon has been widely known to encourage creativity and innovation among its participants. And not only that, this event provides them a platform to showcase those skills. Being a part of this event with us might help you get some brilliant future employees.
