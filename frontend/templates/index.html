Domains

BlockChain technology

Blockchain is a shared, immutable ledger that facilitates the process of recording transactions and tracking assets in a business network. It is majorly used in the transactions involving cryptocurrency.

AI/ML

Artificial intelligence (AI) is intelligence that reads, analyses and synthesizes data. It is an intelligence that is demonstrated by machines. Machine learning (MI) is a subset of AI.

Healthcare

Healthcare is a dynamic subject. With changes in lifestyle of people and varied localities, forms of healthcare and it's accessibility demands innovation and refinement.

Education

During COVID-19 pandemic when schools and colleges were closed, to ensure smooth flow of education, great advancements in technology were made. Meeting platforms, educational platforms helped millions of students study during the tough times and new innovations are done nearly every day.

Open Innovation

Open Innovation is much like an open source arrangement, Businesses and organizations tend to share knowledge and information about their problems hoping to get an innovative solution from people all over the world irrespective of the fact that if they're an employee of the company.

Why Sponsor Us?

Exposure to Young, Intelligent and Brilliant Minds

Nirma University has one of the best pool of enthusiastic student developers who are always ready to showcase their talents. Supporting this event will not only be a great marketing activity but also a super motivating factor for the students.

Advertising your product to over a 500 participants

In today's social media world, students have the best connections over several applications. Hack-NU-Thon is one of the best way to promote the company among students of Nirma University and other participating universities.

A chance to promote innovation and opportunities

Over the years, HackNUthon has been widely known to encourage creativity and innovation among its participants. And not only that, this event provides them a platform to showcase those skills. Being a part of this event with us might help you get some brilliant future employees.
